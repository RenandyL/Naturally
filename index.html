What does it mean to have natural hair?

African Americans have a long history of applying chemicals to their hair, it can be anything from relaxer, perm and even texturizer. A relaxer straightens the hair and alters the natural hair texture. A perm curls straightened hair to make it curly and a texturizer loosens tight curls. To be natural means not applying those chemicals to your hair.


You will be allowing your hair to grow naturally out of your scalp without altering it. There are two possible ways of going natural, transitioning or big chop. Transitioning is simply growing your chemical and processed hair out by wearing styles like twists and bantu knots, braids outs and protective styles until the hair is all one texture.
